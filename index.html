How PPG is calculated
Strong production (PPG > 2): 75–85% actual + 15% career max + 0–10% model projection
Limited production (PPG > 0): 50% actual + 35% career max + 15% model projection
No production (inactive): 60% career max + 40% model projection
Model projection = predicted career max PPG from college stats model. Applied at 10% for 4yr players, 5% for 3yr, 0% for 1–2yr. Actual production always dominates where available.
